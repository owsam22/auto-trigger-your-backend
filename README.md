# TriggerPulse ⚡

**TriggerPulse** is a professional backend monitoring and wake-up service designed to keep your "sleeping" servers (like those on Render or Heroku) alive and responsive. 

### 🌐 Live Links
- **App**: [https://auto-trigger-your-backend.vercel.app](https://auto-trigger-your-backend.vercel.app) 


---

### 🚀 How to Use
1.  **Register/Login**: Create your account on the portal to get started.
2.  **Submit your URL**: Add your backend's health check or trigger endpoint (e.g., `https://your-api.com/ping`).
3.  **wait for Approval**: Once submitted, our admins will review and activate your URL.
4.  **Stay Alive**: Once approved, the system automatically triggers your URL **every 10 minutes** to ensure it never goes back to sleep.

---



### 🔥 Key Features
- **Instant Activation**: Your first trigger happens immediately upon admin approval.
- **Optimized 10-Min Cycle**: Staggered triggering ensures your backends are stayed awake without overloading our servers.
- **Smart Tracking**: Monitor "Alive" vs "Down" statuses directly from your clean, light-themed dashboard.
